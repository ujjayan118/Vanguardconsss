WHO WE ARE

Founded in 1995, Vanguard Construction has grown from a regional builder to an international construction leader. Our team of architects, engineers, and craftsmen combine cutting-edge technology with time-honored building techniques to create structures that stand the test of time.

We specialize in sustainable construction practices, with over 85% of our projects achieving LEED certification. Our commitment to safety, quality, and client satisfaction has earned us numerous industry awards and a reputation for excellence.
